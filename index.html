Born on May 15, 1997, in the serene city of Lalitpur Thuladurlung, Nepal, I embarked on a journey that would shape my future. Growing up in this culturally rich environment, I developed a deep appreciation for knowledge and a curiosity to explore the world around me.

My educational journey began at Shree Bajra Barahi Higher Secondary School, where I completed my high school education. During those formative years, I discovered a passion for physics. The intricacies of the subject fascinated me, and I knew I wanted to delve deeper into its mysteries. With this aspiration in mind, I enrolled in Tri Chandra Multiple Campus, a prestigious institution renowned for its academic excellence. At the campus, I chose to major in physics, immersing myself in the world of equations, experiments, and scientific theories. I relished the challenges and the opportunity to expand my understanding of the universe.

However, in 2018, fate presented me with an unexpected opportunity. An invitation to the United States opened new doors and possibilities. Driven by a thirst for knowledge and a desire to explore different horizons, I made the difficult decision to temporarily pause my bachelor's degree at Tri Chandra Multiple Campus. Stepping foot in the United States, I found myself in a completely new environment. Everything was different, from the culture to the language, and I faced various challenges in acclimating to this unfamiliar territory. Adjusting to the changes, I decided to dedicate the first year to acclimating to my new surroundings, understanding the local customs, and improving my language skills. As I settled into this new chapter of my life, I realized the importance of adapting and persevering in the face of adversity. I was determined not to let the circumstances hinder my pursuit of knowledge. In pursuit of my passion, I joined Richland College, where I decided to explore the field of computer science.

Enrolling as a computer science major, I embraced the world of coding, algorithms, and software development. The logical thinking required in computer science complemented my analytical mindset nurtured by my physics background. Through rigorous coursework, hands-on projects, and collaborative experiences, I honed my skills and discovered a new passion within the realm of technology. After completing my associate degree in computer science at Richland College in 2022, I was eager to continue my educational journey and broaden my horizons further. Guided by my unwavering commitment to learning, I made the decision to transfer to the University of Texas at Dallas, renowned for its excellence in computer science education. Now, as a student at the University of Texas at Dallas, I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science, eagerly embracing the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. I am excited to engage in advanced coursework, collaborate with fellow passionate individuals, and delve deeper into the world of computer science.

My journey from Nepal to the United States has shaped me into a resilient and adaptable individual. It has taught me the value of embracing change, pursuing my passions, and overcoming obstacles along the way. With each step, I have grown academically, intellectually, and personally, and I am grateful for the opportunities that have come my way. As I strive to earn my bachelor's degree in computer science, I am filled with a sense of anticipation and determination. I am confident that the skills, knowledge, and experiences I gain throughout this journey will empower me to contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving field of technology and make a positive impact on the world around me.
